The Ophelia Syndrome

Read Diagnosing and Treating the Ophelia Syndrome. Please time yourself and keep track of about how long, in minutes, it takes for you to read this article. Please don't hurry and please don't guess. This isn't a race. Read to understand instead of hurrying.

After completing the reading survey, read The Divine Law of Learning by Elder Kerr.

These two articles reinforce each other. Ponder deeply about in what ways you may currently be Ophelia. Also, ponder deeply how you might need to change in order to meet what Elder Kerr states is The Lord's desire for your education.

Write your conclusions and any of your plans for change in your personal, self-reflective, meta-cognative journal for this course. Be honest and specific. The instructor will never see and will never ask for what you write in your journal.
