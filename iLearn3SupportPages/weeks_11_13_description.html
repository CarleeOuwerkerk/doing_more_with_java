Weeks 11 - 13

Working Professionally

In these weeks you will continue to work to the plan you created with your team. If you have been assessed at a 'Disagree' level of fluency in all the technical areas of the course before the beginning of week 11, you and your team can work to your team plan to achieve fluency assessments of 'Agree' or 'Strongly Agree'. If you haven't been assessed as having a 'Disagree' level of fluency in all the tech, continue working towards that goal. Detailed information about how to achieve 'Agree' or 'Strongly Agree' fluency assessments is available in the Course module. Please look there for specifics.

Objectives

  • Design a personal application that uses the technologies in the course.
  • Meet with your team to assist one another.
  • Prepare and submit fluency evidence documents to achieve Agree or Strongly Agree fluencies (This will not happen every week. It will happen according to your team schedule).
