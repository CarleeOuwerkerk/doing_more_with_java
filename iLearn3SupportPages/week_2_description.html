Week 2

Getting Connected and Start Working

This week you will join a team and work with them to create your team schedule for the course. There is a description of when you need to complete different portions of the course in the links that are in the Course module. Please look there for specifics. Feel free to pick any agile methodology you, as a team, would like. Use it to create your schedule. You can find a list of some agile methodologies in the Tech Helps link in the Course module.

Objectives

  • Meet and interact with your team.
  • Decide on an agile methodology your team will use.
  • Based on the methodology your team chooses, create a team schedule for achieving the fluency you want.
  • Begin working toward your schedule.
