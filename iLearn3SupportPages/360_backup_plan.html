Backup Plan - CIT 360

At the beginning of each semester, perform the following steps.

  1. In your web browser go to the CIT 360 gitHub repository
  2. Click on the green "Clone or download" button on the right hand side of the screen.
  3. Click on the "Download Zip" option at the bottom of the popup window.
  4. Wait for the 'doing_more_with_java-master.zip' file to complete downloading.
  5. Move the downloaded file to a location determined by your boss.
