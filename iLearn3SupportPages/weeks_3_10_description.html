Weeks 3 - 10

Working Consistantly and Professionally

In these weeks you will work to the plan you created with your team. Remember, professionals do not ignore dates they have committed to. If you are unable to meet a commitment date work with your team to update your team's schedule. Do not ignore a commitment date. Detailed information about what you need to achieve in these weeks is available in the Course module. Please look there for specifics about the end of week 10 and what you will be doing in weeks 11 - 13.

Objectives

  • Work toward your schedule.
  • Meet with your team to teach one another and adjust the team schedule.
  • Prepare and submit fluency evidence documents to achieve Disagree fluencies (This will not happen every week. It will happen according to your team schedule).
  • About the end of week 9, start thinking about an app you might want to create.
