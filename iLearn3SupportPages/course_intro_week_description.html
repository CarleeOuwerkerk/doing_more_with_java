Course Introduction Week

Welcome

Welcome to class. There's a lot to do and learn this week that will help you be successful in the class. Reserve several hours this week to complete the objectives listed below. You will find instructions on how to accomplish these outcomes in the links that are in the Course module. Please look there for secific instructions.

Objectives

  • Gain deep insights to the structure of this course and your responsibilities by reading and understanding the documents in the Course module.
  • Start a personal, private, self-reflective/meta-cognative journal.
  • Come to understand if you have been Ophelia in your education by reading and understanding an article on the Ophelia Syndrome.
  • Set up all accounts required for the course and understand how to use them.
  • Use a Google Hangout to introduce yourself to the other students. Include in your introduction a few observations you've made regarding the Ophelia syndrome or some things Elder Kerr taught. Post your Google Hangout video to YouTube and to the class Google+ community. Feel free to listen to some of your classmates' introduction videos.
